About This System

analytics.Cyber Threat Detection and Prediction Using Machine Learning is a comprehensive cybersecurity solution that leverages machine learning techniques to enhance threat detection, risk assessment, and predictive analysis. The system is designed to automatically identify suspicious activities, detect cyberattacks, and forecast emerging threats before they occur. It integrates data preprocessing, threat classification, anomaly detection, predictive analytics, and reporting modules into a unified platform. A built-in SOC dashboard provides real-time monitoring, alert management, threat visualization, and security reporting, allowing organizations to strengthen their cybersecurity posture and respond proactively to evolving cyber threats
